🏫 Žiacke (pseudonymné) kontá a triedy

Učitelia môžu v LexiNove vytvárať triedy, do ktorých sa žiaci pridávajú kódom triedy. Aby sme minimalizovali spracúvanie osobných údajov detí (čl. 8 GDPR), žiaci sa môžu pridať bez e-mailu — len s prezývkou a heslom (pseudonymné konto).

  • Čo zbierame — pri pseudonymnom konte iba zvolenú prezývku, heslo (ako bezpečný hash) a výsledky učenia. Žiadny e-mail, meno ani iné identifikačné údaje. Odporúčame používať prezývku, z ktorej nie je možné žiaka identifikovať (nie celé meno).
  • Čo vidí učiteľ — len pokrok na sadách priradených triede: prezývku, poslednú aktivitu, počet testov, úspešnosť a zvládnutie sád triedy. Súkromné sady a ostatnú aktivitu žiaka učiteľ nevidí.
  • Heslo — učiteľ môže žiakovi heslo resetovať (nastaviť nové), nikdy ho však nevidí.
  • Mazanie — žiak môže konto kedykoľvek zmazať v profile; učiteľ môže žiaka odstrániť z triedy. Pri zmazaní triedy sa pseudonymné kontá bez iného členstva zmažú aj s výsledkami.

🔗 Third parties

  • AI providers — when you use the AI category creation features, only the content you supply yourself and the selected languages are sent to the provider you choose. Depending on the feature this is:
    • AI from a topic — the text you enter (topic/prompt);
    • AI from a photo — the image you upload (photo or screenshot);
    • AI from a video — only the link (URL) to a public YouTube video. We neither download nor store the video; Google fetches it directly. This feature uses Google Gemini exclusively.

  • Session cookie — essential for login. Valid for 30 days. The application cannot function without this cookie.
  • localStorage — stores preferences (dark mode, language) and offline word cache. Remains in browser until manually cleared.
ℹ️ We do not use analytics or advertising cookies (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.).

🗄️ Data retention

  • Account data (email, name, words, categories, test results) — kept for the entire lifetime of your account.
  • After account deletion — all your data including categories and words is immediately and irreversibly removed from our database.
  • Contact messages (via the form) — kept for at most 12 months after the request is resolved, then deleted. When you delete your account, messages sent from your email are deleted too.
  • Backups — database backups at our provider (Supabase) rotate per its policy; deleted data is purged from backups within the normal cycle.

🏫 Student (pseudonymous) accounts and classes

Teachers can create classes in LexiNova that students join using a class code. To minimise the processing of children's personal data (Art. 8 GDPR), students can join without an email — with just a nickname and password (a pseudonymous account).

  • What we collect — for a pseudonymous account only the chosen nickname, a secure password hash and learning results. No email, name or other identifying data. We recommend using a nickname that does not identify the student (not their full name).
  • What the teacher sees — only progress on the sets assigned to the class: nickname, last activity, number of tests, success rate and mastery of class sets. The teacher cannot see the student's private sets or other activity.
  • Password — the teacher can reset a student's password (set a new one) but can never read it.
  • Deletion — a student can delete the account at any time in their profile; a teacher can remove a student from the class. When a class is deleted, pseudonymous accounts with no other class membership are deleted along with their results.
